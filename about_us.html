Techno Cultural Club is a technology based club in school of information and technology that provides GBU students opportunities across all domains to engage with companies by participating in hackathons, quizzes, competitions,etc. It helps students to learn new skills, showcase their talent, gain CV points and eventually get recognized, rewarded and hired. This time Techno Cultural Club brings Research by Learning Hackathon.
